MYO Motion

Project Overview

MyoFlex is a custom-designed wearable system that integrates single-channel electromyography (EMG) with 6-axis inertial motion sensing to simultaneously capture muscle activity and limb movement. The device is built around the Flexiwave EMG sensor by Brainovative Labs and a compact IMU, enabling real-time bio-signal acquisition in a portable and user-friendly form factor.

Designed with a focus on wearability, rapid setup, and signal reliability, MyoFlex features a snap-on electrode mechanism that ensures consistent electrode placement while minimizing setup time. The compact design allows it to be comfortably used in both controlled lab environments and real-world scenarios.

Key Capabilities

  • Single-channel EMG acquisition for muscle activation analysis.
  • 6-axis IMU (accelerometer + gyroscope) for precise motion tracking.
  • Wearable, lightweight, and compact hardware design.
  • Quick and secure snap-electrode system for improved usability.

Interactive Demonstration

To validate and showcase the system’s real-time performance, a custom Flappy Bird game was developed in Unity, controlled entirely using muscle signals from MyoFlex. This interactive application demonstrated reliable EMG-based control and highlighted the device’s potential in human–computer interaction (HCI) and assistive technologies.
