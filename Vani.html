VANI Silent Speech Device

Project Details

The VANI project involved researching and prototyping a Silent Speech Recognition device specifically designed for laryngeal patients to restore their ability to communicate[cite: 6].

By engineering the Flexiwave EMG sensor, I achieved a 10% increase in biomechanics accuracy[cite: 6]. This innovation secured 10 lakh in grants through DST NIDHI Prayas and an additional 6 lakh from the Ministry of Education[cite: 7, 8]. The project was also recognized as a winner in the National Innovation Contest 2020[cite: 20].
