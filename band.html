GPS-Enabled Safety Band for Alzheimer’s Patients

Project Overview

A wearable safety band designed to enhance the safety and independence of Alzheimer’s patients by addressing challenges such as memory loss, time confusion, and disorientation.

The device features real-time GPS tracking to continuously monitor patient location, significantly reducing the risk of wandering or getting lost. An integrated two-way communication system allows patients to instantly contact their caregivers, ensuring timely assistance during emergencies.

Caregivers can remotely access location data and communicate with patients, enabling swift intervention and providing peace of mind. Overall, the solution improves patient safety while supporting greater autonomy in daily life.

Key Features

  • Real-Time GPS Tracking: Continuously tracks patient location to prevent wandering and ensure rapid recovery if the patient is lost.
  • Two-Way Communication: Allows patients to directly contact caregivers through the wearable band.
  • Remote Caregiver Monitoring: Caregivers can access location data and communicate with patients from anywhere.
  • Emergency Support: Enables quick response and intervention in critical situations.

Impact & Applications

This solution enhances patient safety while reducing caregiver anxiety, making it suitable for:

  • Home-based Alzheimer’s care.
  • Assisted living and memory-care facilities.
  • Geriatric patient monitoring systems.
  • Remote and long-term caregiving scenarios.
