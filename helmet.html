Smart Bicycle helmet

Project Overview

This project was especially meaningful to me as it was my first hands-on implementation of a hybrid hardware–IoT concept, combining safety, connectivity, and real-world usability.

The helmet was designed to enhance cyclist safety and visibility, particularly during night rides and urban commuting. It integrates multiple smart features directly into the helmet without compromising comfort or wearability.

Key Capabilities

  • Integrated Night Lights for improved visibility in low-light conditions.
  • Smart Turn Indicators to signal direction changes.
  • Automatic Braking Lights that activate during deceleration.
  • Bluetooth Communication System for wireless control and connectivity.

Journey

This project helped me build a strong foundation in embedded systems, IoT devices, sensor integration, and rapid prototyping, and sparked my long-term interest in assistive and safety-focused technologies. Looking back, this helmet represents an important milestone in my journey—turning ideas into functional, real-world solutions.
