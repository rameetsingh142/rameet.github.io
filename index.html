About Me

I am currently a Biodesign Fellow at AIIMS Delhi, where I utilize the "Identify, Invent, Implement" methodology to validate unmet clinical needs[cite: 7, 9]. My work focuses on developing affordable, commercially viable healthcare solutions through deep clinical immersion[cite: 9].

As the Founder and CEO of Brainovative Labs, I have led the development of the VANI Silent Speech Recognition Device and the Flexiwave EMG sensor[cite: 16, 18]. My entrepreneurial journey has been supported by significant grants, including 10 lakhs from DST NIDHI Prayas and 6 lakhs from the Ministry of Education[cite: 19, 20].

My research background includes a tenure as a Junior Research Fellow at IIT Delhi, where I designed smart rehabilitation gloves for stroke recovery[cite: 10, 14]. This project involved using EMG, IMU, and haptic feedback to assist patients with upper limb disabilities, with testing conducted at AIIMS New Delhi[cite: 14, 15].

I have a passion for creating assistive technology, evidenced by projects like Smart EOG Glasses for photography via blinking and GPS-enabled Alzheimer Bands for caregiver communication[cite: 49, 52]. My portfolio also includes posture-correction garments and sign-to-speech translation systems[cite: 50, 54].

Beyond technical development, I am a Core Team Member at NeuroTechX India, where I have driven a 30% increase in community participation through strategic outreach[cite: 24, 27]. I am dedicated to fostering innovation, having sponsored events like Hack VSIT 5.0 to support the tech community[cite: 21].

With an MCA from Bharati Vidyapeeth and a BCA from VIPS, I combine a strong academic foundation with hands-on expertise in Signal Processing and Embedded Systems[cite: 5, 42]. I am driven by the goal of merging engineering and medicine to improve patient outcomes globally[cite: 9, 23].
