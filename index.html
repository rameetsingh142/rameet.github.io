About Me

I’m a healthcare technology researcher and biodesign fellow at AIIMS Delhi, focused on translating unmet clinical needs into affordable, scalable medical devices. My work spans biomedical sensing, embedded systems, and human-centered design, with hands-on experience in neurorehabilitation, assistive wearables, and biosignal processing . Previously, I was a Junior Research Fellow at IIT Delhi, developing smart rehabilitation systems for stroke patient. I’m also the founder of Brainovative Labs, leading nationally awarded projects in silent speech recognition and biomechanics sensing, supported by government innovation grants. I hold an MCA and BCA, and I’m driven to build clinically validated, real-world healthcare solutions at the intersection of research, engineering, and product development.
