01)

VANI Silent Speech Recognition Device

VANI is a Silent Speech Recognition (SSR) device designed to restore communication for laryngeal and speech-impaired patients by translating silent articulatory muscle movements into audible speech. The system leverages advanced Electromyography (EMG) to capture muscle activation patterns associated with speech production, even in the absence of vocal sound.

02)

MYO MOTION

MyoFlex is a custom-designed wearable system that integrates single-channel electromyography (EMG) with 6-axis inertial motion sensing to simultaneously capture muscle activity and limb movement. The device is built around the Flexiwave EMG sensor by Brainovative Labs and a compact IMU, enabling real-time bio-signal acquisition in a portable and user-friendly form factor.

03)

Smart EOG Glasses

Developed an interactive wearable project using the BIOAmp EXG Pill by Upside Down Labs that enables hands-free photography through eye blinks. The system leverages Electrooculography (EOG) to detect intentional eye blink patterns and trigger image capture.

04)

Flexiwave EMG Sensor

The Brainovative Flexiwave is an advanced Electromyography (EMG) sensor designed to deliver accurate, reliable, and adaptable muscle signal acquisition for next-generation physiological monitoring applications.

05)

Posture Correction T-shirt

Developed a smart posture-correcting wearable t-shirt that combines IMU-based posture tracking with real-time haptic feedback to help users maintain proper posture throughout daily activities.

06)

Sign-to-Speech Glove

Developed a Sign-to-Speech Smart Glove that translates hand gestures and sign language into audible speech, enabling more accessible communication for individuals with speech and hearing impairments.

07)

QR Pulse Oximeter

Designed an enhanced pulse oximeter that not only measures blood oxygen saturation (SpO₂) and pulse rate, but also provides proactive health guidance during critical situations.

08)

Alzheimer Communication Band

Developed a wearable safety band designed to enhance the safety, independence, and quality of life of Alzheimer’s patients .

09)

Smart Safety Helmet

This project was especially meaningful to me as it was my first hands-on implementation of a hybrid hardware–IoT concept, combining safety, connectivity, and real-world usability. The helmet was designed to enhance cyclist safety and visibility, particularly during night rides and urban commuting. It integrates multiple smart features directly into the helmet without compromising comfort or wearability.
