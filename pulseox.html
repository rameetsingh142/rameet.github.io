Smart Pulse Oximeter with QR

Project Overview

Designed an enhanced pulse oximeter that not only measures blood oxygen saturation (SpO₂) and pulse rate, but also provides proactive health guidance during critical situations.

The device functions as a standard pulse oximeter under normal conditions. When SpO₂ levels fall below 95%, it automatically displays a flashing QR code on the screen to alert the user.

Scanning the QR code redirects users to verified medical resources, offering immediate guidance, recommended actions, and next steps to manage low oxygen levels. This approach ensures timely access to trusted information without panic or delay.

Key Features

  • Accurate measurement of SpO₂ and pulse rate.
  • Automatic QR code alert triggered during hypoxia.
  • Direct access to verified medical guidance.
  • Designed for proactive, user-centric health support.

Impact

By combining monitoring with instant information access, the device goes beyond passive measurement and supports informed decision-making during emergencies, making it especially useful for home monitoring, elderly care, and remote health scenarios.
